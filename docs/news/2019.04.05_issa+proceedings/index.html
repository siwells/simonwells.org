Paper Published in the Proceedings of ISSA 2018

  • Apr 5, 2019

Together with my colleague, Al Baker, I published a paper in the proceedings of the 9th Conference of the International Society for the Study of Argumentation hosted by the University of Amsterdam. Our paper is on the theme of deception and intelligent machines. More specifically, we investigated som of the question of when a machine should lie or otherwise decieve during interaction with people.

  • Trusting Intelligent Machines: Deepening Trust Within Socio-Technical Systems
