SADN presentation at CMNA'25

  • Dec 12, 2025

Today I presented my latest paper, joint work with Mark Snaith, on the Simple Argument Description Notation (SADN) to the CMNA community at the 25th annual international workshop on Computational Models of Natural Argument (CMNA'25).

This talk, and it’s associated paper, introduce the Simple Argument Description Notation (SADN), a set of commands for the SADFace REPL to enable declarative text-user-interface (TUI) driven construction and manipulation of argument structures.
