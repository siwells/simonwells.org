24th International Workshop on Computational Models of Natural Argument

  • Sep 17, 2024

The 24th annual international workshop on Computational Models of Natural Argument (CMNA'24) will run on 17th September in Hagen, Germany. This year, in line with the peripatetic tradition of CMNA, we will be hosted by the 10th International Conference on Computational Models of Argument (COMMA 2024).

This year we have a really nice lineup of papers covering many interesting topics from within the CMNA remit. The programme is available and all contributed papers are archived in CEUR Workshop proceedings volume #3769.
